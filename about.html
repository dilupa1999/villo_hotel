About Villo Hotels

Where Sri Lankan Hospitality Meets Modern Sophistication

Located in the heart of Sri Lanka’s cultural capital, Villo Hotels Kandy offers a seamless blend of timeless tradition and contemporary comfort. Designed for the modern traveler, our hotel is a celebration of warm hospitality, thoughtful luxury, and breathtaking views of the sacred city.
Whether you're here to explore Kandy’s rich heritage or simply relax in refined surroundings, Villo Hotels promises an experience that’s both authentic and unforgettable.

Our Journey

2005

Founded in 2005 as a boutique 12-room property, Villo Hotels quickly became known for personalized service and attention to detail.

2010

We expanded to 30 rooms and introduced our award-winning spa.

2015

Marked our 10th anniversary with a complete renovation and the launch of our rooftop infinity pool.

2020

Proudly received Green Globe Certification for our sustainability efforts.

2023

Honored as 'Best Luxury Hotel in Kandy' for the third consecutive year by Sri Lanka Tourism Awards.
