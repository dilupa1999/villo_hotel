About Villo Hotels Kandy

You can make reservations through our website, by calling our reservation hotline at +94 81 222 3333, or by emailing reservations@villohotelskandy.com. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak season (December-April) and during cultural festivals.

We're located within walking distance of the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and Kandy Lake. The Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya, Ceylon Tea Museum, and Udawattakele Forest Reserve are all within a short drive. We offer guided cultural tours to help you explore these magnificent sites.

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfer services from Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) in Colombo. The journey takes approximately 3 hours through scenic Sri Lankan countryside. We also arrange transfers from domestic airports and railway stations in Kandy.

Our signature restaurant serves authentic Sri Lankan cuisine alongside international dishes. We offer traditional rice and curry buffets, fresh seafood, and vegetarian options. Room service is available 24/7, and we can arrange special dietary requirements with advance notice.
