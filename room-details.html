Delux Rooms

Room Description

Our Deluxe Mountain View Suite offers the perfect blend of luxury and comfort, designed to make your stay in Kandy unforgettable.

Spanning 45 square meters, this spacious suite features a king-size bed with premium linens, a separate sitting area, and a private balcony that offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills. The elegant decor combines traditional Sri Lankan elements with modern amenities to create a sophisticated retreat.

The marble-appointed bathroom includes both a rainfall shower and a deep soaking tub, complemented by premium bath amenities. Floor-to-ceiling windows allow natural light to flood the space while offering stunning vistas of Kandy's lush landscape.
